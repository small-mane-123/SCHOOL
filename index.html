Who Are We?

Baytul-ilm Academy is a famous Arabic language training Institute which majors in virtual training with the intent of easing the seemingly
difficult most influential and leading language spoken by human race and taking the acquisition of the language to their door steps and in the comfort
of their homes and offices.

The Academy started during the Covid-19 pandemic when the leadership of The Muslim Congress, Lagos State, Nigeria thought of how Muslims can
make the best of the National and international lockdowns which brought the entire world to its knees.

Our Values

Our Mission

To facilitate the restoration of Arabic as the language of Muslims and ensure unification of Islamic creed.

Our Vision

To be the leading online and physical Arabic language training Institute in Africa.

Our Objectives

1. To promote Arabic literacy as the language of Islam and Muslims worldwide.
2. To develop capacity of Muslims in the recitation and understanding of the Qur'an and the Prophetic Tradition.
3. To train and produce competent scholars and du'aat to the path of Allah.
4. To create a virtual space opportunity to learn and understand Islam and Arabic language.
