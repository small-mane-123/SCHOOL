About Us

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

Baytul-ilm Academy is a famous Arabic
language training Institute which majors in
virtual training with the intent of easing the
seemingly difficult most influential and leading
language spoken by human race and taking the
acquisition of the language to their door steps and
in the comfort of their homes and offices.

The Academy started during the Covid-19
pandemic when the leadership of The Muslim
Congress, Lagos State, Nigeria thought of how
Muslims can make the best of the National and
international lockdowns which brought the entire
world to its knees.

Baytul-ilm Academy has grown from strength to
strength in terms of number of enrolled students,
facilitators and other stakeholders of the Academy
to the glory of Allah.

Baytul-ilm can today boast of graduates from the
Institute who today represent Islam with Islamic and
Arabic language knowledge effectively and efficiently in
their various life endeavours.

The Academy as a training institute running part of
its activities as virtual, has students from various
continents of the world among which are Europe,
America, Asia, Middle East.

With dedicated Board and management staff,
Fushaa is an Arabic language training centre to
beat by others and it gives this glory to Allah
(SWT).

The Institute has strategic plans spanning through
short, medium and long term plans to expand her
activities to reach of all and sundry.
